# AI Lead Qualification

**Type:** Freelance / Side Project
**Stack:** GPT-4, API integrations, automation workflows

## Overview

AI-powered lead qualification system using GPT-4 to automatically score, categorize, and prioritize inbound leads. Replaced manual lead review with intelligent triage that analyzed company fit, intent signals, and deal potential.

## Technical Details

- Integrated GPT-4 API for natural language analysis of lead data
- Built scoring model considering firmographic data, engagement signals, and stated needs
- Automated routing of qualified leads to appropriate sales representatives
- Generated qualification summaries for sales team context
