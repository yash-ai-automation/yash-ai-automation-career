# Enterprise Engineering

Building software systems at scale: high transaction volumes, strict uptime requirements, microservices architecture, CI/CD pipelines, and production-grade reliability. This is the engineering foundation Yash brings from 6+ years at [[bell-canada]], [[morningstar]], and [[virtusa]].

## What Enterprise Engineering Means

Unlike prototype or startup-scale work, enterprise engineering demands:

- **High throughput**: Handling millions of transactions daily without degradation
- **Reliability**: 99.9%+ uptime SLAs enforced by real business consequences
- **Observability**: Monitoring, alerting, and dashboards for proactive issue detection
- **Security**: Authentication (JWT, OAuth/OIDC), data encryption, compliance
- **Maintainability**: CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, code review processes
- **Team discipline**: Cross-functional collaboration, SDLC standards, Jira-based tracking
