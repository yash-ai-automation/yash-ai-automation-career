# AI Automation

The discipline of using artificial intelligence to automate business processes -- replacing manual, repetitive work with intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and scale. This is Yash Anghan's core positioning and primary career identity.

## What It Means in Practice

AI automation goes beyond simple rule-based workflows (if-this-then-that). It combines:

- **Traditional workflow automation** tools ([[workflow-orchestration]]) like [[n8n]] and [[make-com]] for multi-step process design
- **AI/ML models** ([[generative-ai]], [[mlops-llmops]]) for intelligent decision-making within those workflows
- **Enterprise-grade engineering** ([[enterprise-engineering]]) for reliability, error handling, and scale

The result: systems that not only execute tasks but understand context, classify data, generate outputs, and improve over time.
