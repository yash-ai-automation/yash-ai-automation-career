# Deal-Breakers

Hard exclusion criteria for Yash Anghan's job search. If a role hits any of these, mark it SKIP and do not apply. These filters save time for both Yash and the recruiter.

## Hard Deal-Breakers

### 1. US-Only Work Authorization
Roles requiring US citizenship, US permanent residency, or US work authorization. Yash is a **Canadian Permanent Resident** with no US work authorization.

**How to detect**: JD says "must be authorized to work in the United States," "US citizens only," "no visa sponsorship (US)," or location is US-only with no remote-Canada option.

### 2. Five-Day On-Site Requirement
Fully in-office, 5 days per week. Yash prefers hybrid and is open to some on-site, but 5-day mandatory office is excluded.

**How to detect**: JD says "fully on-site," "in-office 5 days," or location section specifies no remote/hybrid.

### 3. Security Clearance Required
Roles requiring Reliability, Secret, or Top Secret Canadian security clearance (or US equivalents). Yash does not hold security clearance.

**How to detect**: JD mentions "security clearance required," "must have Secret clearance," or is in government/defence sectors requiring clearance.

### 4. Below $90K CAD
Roles with stated compensation below $90,000 CAD annually. This is the hard floor -- see [[compensation]] for the full range.

**How to detect**: JD states salary range with maximum below $90K. If salary is not listed, do not exclude on this basis alone.

### 5. Internships
Yash has 6+ years of experience and a Master's degree. Internship-level roles are underscoped.

**How to detect**: Title contains "Intern," "Co-op," or "Junior" with internship-level compensation.

### 6. Director+ / Principal Level
Over-scoped roles requiring 10+ years of management experience. Yash targets IC and senior IC roles, not executive leadership.

**How to detect**: Title contains "Director," "VP," "Principal," "Head of" (unless Head of AI at a small startup), or requires 10+ years management experience.

## Soft Concerns (Not Deal-Breakers, But Flag)

These don't automatically exclude a role but should be noted in evaluation:

- **French language required**: Yash speaks English only. Federal/Quebec roles requiring bilingual EN/FR are poor fits
- **Relocation required**: No relocation -- Toronto/GTA only (see [[location-preferences]])
- **Heavy travel (50%+)**: Maximum 25% travel
- **Overtime/weekends required**: Not willing
- **Extremely early stage (pre-seed)**: Prefers Series A+ (see [[form-fill-data]])
- **No AI component**: Pure backend or frontend roles without AI/automation are secondary fits at best
- **Contract/part-time only**: Prefers full-time permanent

## Filtering in Pipeline

The automated pipeline ([[pipeline-spec]]) applies these filters during the Discovery phase:
- Location: Excludes US-only
- Level: Excludes internships, Director+, Principal
- Date: Prefers last 7 days
- Title matching: See [[target-roles]] for included titles
